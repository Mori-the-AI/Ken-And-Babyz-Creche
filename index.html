About Us

Why Parents Love Ken & Babyz

We're dedicated to providing the best early childhood education and care for your little ones.

Home Away From Home

Our warm and inviting spaces make your child feel safe, comfortable, and at ease from the moment they arrive.

Play-Based Learning

We blend education with fun activities that spark curiosity and foster a lifelong love for learning.

Spacious Play Areas

Our thoughtfully designed play areas are spacious, safe, and equipped with age-appropriate toys and equipment.

Healthy Meals

Nutritious, home-cooked meals prepared fresh daily with organic ingredients and dietary accommodations.

Creative Arts

Encouraging creativity through painting, drawing, dancing, music to help children express themselves and develop fine motor skills.

After School Care

Extended care hours to support working parents with supervised play, homework help, and structured activities.

Sleepovers

Organized sleepovers for children of all ages to aid Travelling and working parents

A Message from Our CEO

Kehinde Oladipo

Founder & CEO

"Every child deserves a nurturing environment where they can explore, learn, and grow. When I founded Ken and Babyz Creche, my vision was simple: create a place that feels like an extension of home, where children are loved, respected, and empowered to become the best versions of themselves."

With over 15 years of experience in early childhood education and child psychology, I've dedicated my life to understanding how young minds develop and thrive. As a mother of four myself, I know firsthand the joys and challenges of parenting, and I bring that empathy and insight into everything we do at Ken and Babyz.
